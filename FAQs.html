Frequently Asked Questions


I would love to foster, but I don't have the money to buy food and toys for my animals. What can I do?

PACC will provide all the essential items for your foster animal. There is also a foster Facebook page where you can ask if anyone has extra supplies!

What happens if I foster an animal and end up adopting them?

The ultimate goal is to have your animal adopted! Whether it be with you or another family, we love to see the animals stay out of the shelter and into their forever home!

What if I take my animal home and they are having problems adjusting?

We cannot assume that all animals will be perfect in their home. PACC offers their in house trainers, Top Dogs, that help with training such as reactivity, anxiety, aggression, etc.

I'm looking for an animal that is good with kids and my other pets. Would you be able to match me with one?

With over 500 animals in our shelter, we will be able to find one that suits your lifestyle. Our dedicated team knows the animals and all of their fun and awesome quirks and we will be able to match you with one (or more). We also are able to host meet and greets, where you can bring your animal to the play yard and see if they click with one of the shelter animals!

I want to start the process of fostering an animal. What should I do?

You can either check out our animal survey on the tabs at the top or come into PACC and see the animals for yourself. We will walk you through the process and you can leave with your next best friend in the same day.
